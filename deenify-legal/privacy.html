1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Badzlan Bakar ("We," "Us," or "Our"), the developer of the "Deenify Quran Chat" app ("App"), handles information.

2. What Information We Collect

No Personal Data on Our Servers

We do not collect or store personal user data, such as your name, email, or chat history, on our servers. All chat interactions and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Subscription Data

We receive anonymous subscription data (e.g., purchase token, subscription ID) from Google Play to verify your subscription status. We do not receive your name, address, or financial information.

Anonymous Usage Data

We use anonymized usage data via third-party analytics to understand usage patterns and improve the App's performance.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide app functionality: We use the locally stored information on your device to deliver core features.
  • To manage subscriptions: We use anonymous subscription data to manage your access to premium features.
  • To improve the app: We use anonymous usage data to analyze and enhance the App's performance.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any personally identifiable data with third parties. Anonymous data may be shared with analytics providers. Your financial details are handled securely by Google Play.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures for data stored on your device. Since all data is local, the security of that data also depends on your device's security.

6. User Data Rights and Data Deletion

You control all data stored on your device and can delete it by clearing the app's cache or uninstalling it. For subscription management, use the Google Play Subscription Center.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy and will post changes here. Your continued use of the App confirms your acceptance of any changes.
